The origin

Meet our Founder

I was tired of rolling my eyes when I heard views or onions which I knew were simply stereotypical, biased or untrue. But a lot of this was based on my gut feeling and experiences as a Nigerian and I lacked the hard and research backed data to prove that most of these things were fallacies. Additionally, while laying the groundwork for an agricultural and food manufacturing project in Nigeria, I was slapped in all directions by the dearth of factual information needed to drive the project and influence decisions. Initially, I whined and whined about the problem until it hit me! Why not start collating facts and targeted research papers from different fields and topics? We can create our own database of Nigeria and Africa while also making it easily accessible!
